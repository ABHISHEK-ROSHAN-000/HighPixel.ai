Vector is a cutting-edge language model developed by HighPixel.ai, a pioneer in AI research, and Abhishek Roshan, the driving force behind its creation.

Vector is available in various variants, each with a distinct set of parameters, including 35 million (35M), 126 million (126M), and 162 million (162M) parameters, making it a highly optimized model for on-device usage on low-end devices such as mobile phones.

This variant is specifically designed for low-power devices like mobile phones, where high computational requirements can be a significant bottleneck.

The training datasets for Vector are of massive proportions, totaling 50 GB, which is a testament to the model's scalability and adaptability.

Moreover, Vector is an open-source model, allowing developers to tap into its vast capabilities and explore its potential in various applications, from chatbots to content generation.
